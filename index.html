☉ About Satyabrata Nana

Bringing the Sacred Traditions of Odisha to Bengaluru

With over a decade of experience in performing Vedic rituals, Satyabrata Nana is a deeply devoted and knowledgeable Odia Brahmin priest based in Bengaluru. Trained in the traditional gurukul system, he carries the rich spiritual heritage of Odisha to families across Karnataka's capital.

Whether it is a Griha Pravesh, a wedding ceremony, or an annual Shraddha, Satyabrata Nana ensures every ritual is performed with proper Vedic mantras, correct procedures, and heartfelt devotion. He is fluent in Odia, Hindi, English, and Kannada.
